Tracking Unemployment in Idaho

By Chris Amico, Danny DeBelius,
and Matt Stiles

Thousands remain jobless after the 2008 financial crisis, which caused unemployment to spike across the country. The Gem State’s jobless rate is declining, however, and is now lower than the nation as a whole. Use this tool to track unemployment by county over time, read personal accounts of Idahoans still seeking work — and tell us your story. More about this data.
